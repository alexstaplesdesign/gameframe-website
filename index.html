Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

What kind of photos work best?

High-quality, well-lit photos where the athlete is clearly visible work best. Phone photos are perfectly fine! Action shots or posed photos both work great - just avoid heavily blurred or very dark images.

How long does it take?

Most designs are completed within 3-5 business days. You'll receive your high-resolution digital files first, and physical prints ship the same day as approval.

Can I request changes?

Absolutely! One round of revisions is included with every order. We'll work with you to make sure you love your sports card design.

What sports do you cover?

All sports! Baseball, basketball, football, soccer, hockey, tennis, wrestling, gymnastics, and more. Each design is customized for the specific sport and team colors.

What if I'm not satisfied?

Your satisfaction is 100% guaranteed. We'll work with you until you love your design, or provide a full refund if we can't meet your expectations.
